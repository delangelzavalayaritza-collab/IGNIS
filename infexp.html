Experiment Tips 🧪

⚗️ Be curious! — Every question can turn into a fun experiment. Curiosity is your best tool!
🌈 Use colors and reactions — Mix safe materials to see how the world changes before your eyes!
📋 Write your results — Like a true scientist, note what happens after each try.
🧤 Stay safe — Use gloves, goggles, and an adult when doing experiments!
🧠 Think about “why” — Don’t just look at what happens, try to explain the magic behind it.
💥 Try again! — Science loves mistakes. Every fail brings you closer to discovery!
