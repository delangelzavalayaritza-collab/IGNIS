Musical Tips

🥁 Feel the beat — tap your hands or feet to keep the rhythm alive!
🎹 Try different sounds — explore instruments and find your favorite tone.
🎤 Sing loud! — your voice is your first magical instrument.
🎧 Listen carefully — every note tells a story if you pay attention.
🎼 Compose your own tune — even a few notes can make a song that’s truly yours.
🔥 Have fun! — music is joy, energy, and dragon fire for your soul!
